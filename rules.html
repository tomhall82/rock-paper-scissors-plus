Rock, Paper, Scissors

The traditional game of "Rock, Paper, Scissors"

Selct one of the 3 symbols and test your luck!

The rules are as follows:

  • Rock blunts Scissors
  • Paper covers Rock
  • Scissors cut Paper

Rock, Paper, Scissors Plus!

Also known as "Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock", this is a twist on the traditional "Rock, Paper, Scissors" game with a couple of added elements!

Select one of the five symbols to take part in this epic game of chance!

The rules are as follows:

  • Rock blunts Scissors & crushes lizard
  • Paper covers Rock & disproves Spock
  • scissors cut Paper & decapitates Lizard
  • Lizard eats Paper & poisons Spock
  • Spock vaporizes Rock & Spock smashes Scissors
Play Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock

General

Each win will increase the counter showing below the icons but beware, as soon as you lose, your score will be reset to zero!

If you would like to change your Username, please visit the main menu.

At the top of every game screen you will find a shortcuts to switch between game modes, this will reset your highscore.
